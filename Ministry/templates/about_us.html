BACKGROUND OF PBC MINISTRIES

Prophecy Being Fulfilled

The remarkable transformation of Kwi Akuru all miraculously started in the summer of 1976. The Holy Spirit spoke to Aunt Sarah about her 12year old nephew, Jeffrey Vrielink, who had come from Germany to visit his American side of the family in California. She clearly heard God saying that "Jeffrey will someday be a medical missionary to Africa."

There was a 46year period of waiting before God was ready to act. During this period, the parents of the Kwio Akuru community started the Parent's School in 2012. It operated for 8years until the Covid-19 lockdown closed it in 2020. Teachers were sent home without pay, and students had no educational opportunities for nearly 2years. The parents prayed for a miracle. God heard them and in His timing, answered their Prayers.

Fourty-six years after the Prophecy had been given to Aunt Sarah, its finally happened. Uncle Jeff, who had graduated from medical school in 1992, took his first missionary trip to Uganda in July 0f 2022. He felt in love with our country and its people

It was that summer that the Hon.Dr.Betty Udongo Pacutho took a group of American missionaries, including Uncle Jeff, to visit her Ancestral Village of Kwio Akuru. They Worshipped with and prayed for the people of the community and saw healings in Jesus's name. Six weeks after their visit to Kwio Akuru, Uncle Jeff felt compelled by the Holy Spirit to call the Hon. Dr.Betty Udongo and he asked about the needs of this community.

Together they decided to start a new school with new teachers, which Uncle Jeff named "Precious Beloved Children Christian School." In December of 2022, Uncle Jeff returned to Kwio Akuru as team leader with a team of 7 medical students and Dr. Gerald Tumusiime, the dean of medicine from UCU school of medicine in Kampala, where Uncle Jeff is a visiting proffessor of Psychiatry.

In Three days, the team treated over 500 people, and Dr. Gerald Tumusiime performed 3 surgical operations. It was here that Uncle Jeff forged a partnership with "Dr. George" whom Uncle Jeff sponsors every four months to provide free medical care to the pupils of our school. With funding through, true life Bible Church, they have purchased 4 acres of land for the Campus of Gift of God School of Nursing and Widwifery, which they God willing plan to build in Nebbi in 2025.

Initially, Uncle Jeff financed the PRECIOUS BELOVED CHILDREN CHRISTIAN SCHOOL through his private medical practice, Veritas Psychiatry in Grand Rapids, Michigan, USA. After two years,generous donors caught Uncle Jeff's vision and started supporting the school. Students no longer had to study under the trees and get wet during the rainy season.

In 2024, a Church building, a Borehole, the first two school buildings, a medical clinic and a playground were built. The community had prayed for a break-through. God had clearly heard their prayers and provided. True Life Bible Church of Wyoming, Michigan commissioned Uncle Jeff to be their official missionary to Uganda in April of 2024.

They embraced his vision and mission for precious Beloved Children School and Ministries and has been instrumental in helping him build and grow the ministries. Uncle Jeff directs and governs all aspects of PBC Christian School and Ministries.

Under his leadership, the school is growing and our community is thriving, bringing jobs and hope to youths through our work-discipleship programs. Precious Beloved Children Ministries is dedicated to proving quality education , empowering communities, and spreading the love of Christ. Through the empowerment of the Holy Spirit, Uncle Jeff great love for our communities and the support of many generous donors, PBC Ministries continues to transform lives in Kwio Akuru. Among his many initiatives, Uncle Jeff established the Higher Education Scholarship Fund to help qualifying students attains their career goals beyond secondary school.

Guided by Scripture, driven by prayer and led by the Holy Spirit, we strive to:

  1. Proclaim Jesus Christ as Lord and Sviour
  2. Demonstrate God's love through serving others
  3. Build strong, supportive communities
  4. Provide quality education from a Christian worldview
  5. Disciple youths to become readers and citizens in our communities
  6. Provide job opportunities for youths

Our purpose is to transform lives by producing vibrant and Godly communities, fostering a brighter future for generations to come. Our ministry is rooted in Mathew 28:18-12, "Go and make desciples of all nations..."

2.0 VISION AND MISSION STATEMENTS

2.1 Our Vision

Empowering youths, transforming communities, and advancing the Kingdom of God through quality education, spiritual growth, and discipleship programs to shape a brighter future.

2.2 Our Mission

Precious Beloved Children Ministries exists to provide holistic development, education, and spiritual guidance to less privileged youths and children, equipping them with skills, knowledge, and faith to break the cycle of poverty and become change-makers in their communities.

2.3 Aims and Objectives
  1. Provide quality education (primary, secondary, and vocational) to children.
  2. Foster spiritual growth through evangelism, discipleship, and mentorship.
  3. Promote agricultural development and economic empowerment.
  4. Offer technical skills training and employment opportunities.
  5. Organize youth discipleship programs.
  6. Encourage community engagement and social responsibility.
2.4 Core Values
  1. Christ-centeredness: Jesus Christ is our foundation and focus.
  2. Biblical Authority: Scripture guides our beliefs and actions.
  3. Prayerfulness: Prayer is essential to our relationship with God.
  4. Compassion: We demonstrate God's love through serving others.
  5. Unity: We foster unity among Christians and communities.
  6. Evangelism: We share the Gospel with the lost.
  7. Discipleship: We nurture and equip believers for ministry.
  8. Excellence: We strive for academic excellence and a strong work ethic.
  9. Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  10. Spiritual Growth: We prioritize spiritual development.
2.5 Goals for the Children
  1. Spiritual growth and development.
  2. Academic excellence.
  3. Economic empowerment.
  4. Social responsibility and community engagement.
  5. Organize youth discipleship programs.
  6. Emotional intelligence and well-being.
2.6 Target Groups
  1. Orphaned and vulnerable children.
  2. Disadvantaged and unemployed youths.
  3. Children with disabilities.
  4. Children from low-income families.
